About Us

Learn more about our mission and commitment to PWD empowerment

Our Mission

To empower Persons with Disability in Barangay Bulua by providing comprehensive support services, advocating for their rights, and creating inclusive opportunities for their holistic development and self-sufficiency.

Our Vision

A Barrier-free community where every Person with Disability enjoys equal opportunities, full participation, and dignified quality of life, protected by inclusive policies and empowered through sustainable programs.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work
