About

The Bulua Organization (BBPWDDO) is dedicated to providing support, resources, and services to all persons with disabilities in our community. We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities and a fulfilling life.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every PWD receives the assistance they need. From registration to employment programs, we are here for you.

We are committed to promoting inclusion and empowerment in the community through education, advocacy, and responsive support services.
